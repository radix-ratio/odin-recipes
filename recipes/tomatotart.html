Tomato tart

Description

Tart on puff pastry, with ricotta, young onions, garlic and chili peppers

Easy recipe for tomato tart

Ingredients

  • approx. 250 g puff pastry
  • 100 g ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 raw egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • approx. 300 g of various tomatoes (e.g. yellow and red), including a few
  • cherry ones
  • chili peppers
  • young onion
  • fresh basil
  • extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C.
  2. Mix ricotta with egg, 1/2 teaspoon oregano, 1 clove of garlic squeezed through a press, salt and pepper.
  3. Cut the tomatoes into slices, preferably "horizontal", i.e. setting the tomato on its side.
  4. Remove the puff pastry from the refrigerator, unroll it and place it on a baking paper lined sheet and fold the edge of the tart.
  5. Spread the ricotta or spoon it out without spreading, then arrange the tomatoes. Brush them with the second clove of garlic squeezed through a press, sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon of oregano and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Place in the oven and bake until golden brown for about 23 - 25 minutes. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with chopped chili, onion and fresh basil.
