Chocolate Pancakes

Description

Fluffy and instant chocolate pancakes on yogurt

Easy recipe for chocolate pancakes

Ingredients

  • 250 g natural yogurt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 op. vanilla sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)
  • 170 g wheat flour
  • 1 tsp cocoa
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • butter to grease the pan or oil
  • for serving e.g. powdered sugar, maple syrup, fruit, jam

Steps

  1. Remove the yogurt and eggs from the refrigerator beforehand to warm (or gently heat in a bath or microwave).
  2. Mix the yogurt with the eggs, vanilla sugar and sugar using a whisk or fork.
  3. Sift flour with cocoa, baking powder and baking soda into another bowl, mix thoroughly.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix with a whisk or fork gently and mix briefly until the ingredients are combined into a homogeneous mass (you can't mix for too long, just until the ingredients are first combined).
  5. Heat up a frying pan (such as a large pancake pan or any other pan with a non-stick coating), grease it with butter or oil and put 2 flat tablespoons of batter per pancake keeping spaces (maximum 4 pancakes at a time).
  6. Fry on not too high heat until they rise and are nicely browned (about 3 minutes).Flip the pancakes over to the other side and fry until browned, about 3 minutes over moderate heat.
  7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, serve with, for example, maple syrup or jam, fruit.
